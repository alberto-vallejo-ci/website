Hi reader, I'm Alberto (or just Beto as my friends call me) and a Software Engineer. I'm located in a small city near the pacific coast called Colima. Here I've found a warm place surrounded by nature and a lot of friendly people, but also I got the chance to build a career as a Web Developer.
