About Me

Hola! Welcome to my website.

Feel free to look at my work experience or download my resume. I am a Mexican currently living in Toronto, Canada.

I started working as a web developer in 2012, using Ruby on Rails in a Web Consultancy company in one of my favorite cities, Colima, Mexico. Since that moment, I've been fascinated by creating new Web Applications, motivated to try new strategies or frameworks, and of course, the chance to work with amazing people over these years.

Luckily, I've had the chance to work on small projects with just a couple of teammates to work on fast-growing startups with a huge team behind. I've had the pleasure of working with startups from Mexico, the US, and Canada, being part of outstanding teams where I learned a lot and felt each project as my own.

But coding is not everything in my life. When I'm far from the computer, I watch soccer or rugby games, go camping, try new food, or cook, and I also love to ride my motorcycle on country roads with beautiful landscapes.
